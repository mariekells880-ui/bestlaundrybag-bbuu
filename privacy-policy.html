​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

To make things better for you, we grab some info. This is what we're talking about:

a. Info You Give Us Directly

*   Your name, email, or number when you reach out or sign up.
*   Payment or billing stuff when you buy something.
*   Any messages or feedback you send our way.

b. Info We Snag Automatically

*   Your IP address and what kind of browser you're using.
*   What kind of phone or computer you're on.
*   What pages you look at and for how long.
*   Cookies and stuff like that.

Basically, we just grab what we need, nothing extra.

**2. How We Use Your Info**

We use your info to:

*   Keep things running and make them better.
*   Process payments.
*   Let you know about updates.
*   Answer your questions.
*   If the law tells us to.

We might look at all the data as a whole (without picking out individuals) to see how people are using everything.

**3. Cookies and All That**

Cookies are there to help us improve things. They remember what you like and let us see how people are using the site. You can turn them off in your browser, but some things might not work right if you do.

**4. When We Share**

We never sell your data. Seriously. But we might share with:

*   People who help us do things (like hosting the site, processing payments, or sending emails).
*   The authorities if we have to by law.
*   The new owners if we sell the business.

Everyone who gets your data has to keep it safe and only use it for real reasons.

**5. How Long We Keep It and How We Protect It**

We only keep your info as long as we need it for the reasons we got it in the first place. We try to keep your info safe using normal security stuff. But nothing online is perfect, so be careful what you share.

**6. Your Choices**

You can:

*   See or change your info.
*   Ask us to delete your data.
*   Stop getting marketing emails.
*   Say no to certain ways we use your data.


**7. Kids' Privacy**

We don't knowingly grab info from anyone under 13. If you think we messed up, let us know, and we'll delete it ASAP.

**8. Changes to This Policy**

We might change this Privacy Policy sometimes. If we make big changes, we'll let you know on the site or send you an email. Check back every now and then to stay in the loop.
